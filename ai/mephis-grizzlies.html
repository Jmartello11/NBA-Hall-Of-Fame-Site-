Memphis Grizzlies

In 2009 Iverson would sign with the Grizzlies for one year, but would later leave due to his displeasure playing from the bench. He left the team in November of that same year due to "personal reasons." Later that same year the 76ers would persue Iverson due to Lou Willimas suffering a broken jaw.

December 7, 2009 Iverson made his return debut in a loss against his former team the Nuggets. In 2010 he would leave the 76ers citing the need to attend to his 4 year old daughter. His final game was against the Bulls on February 20, 2010.
