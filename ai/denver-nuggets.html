Denver Nuggets

In 2006 he was traded to the Denver Nuggets for Andre Miller and Joe Smith, along with two first round picks. He was paired with Carmelo was was the leading scorer with Iverson being the second league leading scorer. They made the playoffs in his first year, but lost to the Spurs in the first round.

In 2008 Iverson was traded from the Nuggets to the Pistons for Chauncey Billups, Antonio McDyess, and Cheikh Samb. Iverson would lose playing time that year, and would not play in a majority of the 2009 season due to a back injury.
