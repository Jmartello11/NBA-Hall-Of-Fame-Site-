Allen Iverson

One of the most prolific players in the NBA. Iverson played 14 seasons, was an 11 time allstar, 2 time allstar MVP, one time MVP winning in 2001. Iverson was most known for his aggressive playing, alogn with his antics off the court. He was also one of the firts players to wear conrows which would gain popularity in the following years. He was rated a top five for shotting guards alltime by ESPN.
