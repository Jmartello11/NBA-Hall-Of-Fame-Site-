Shaquille O'Neal

Shaquille O'Neal better known as Shaq, was born on March 6th, 1972. Shaq is currently an analyst on thge TV program Insinde the NBA where he joins Ernie Johnson, Kenny Smith, and Charles Barkley to produce one of the most informative and funniest NBA postgame shows. Shaq played for six teams throughout his 19 year NBA Career. Shaq is listed at 7ft 1in at 325 pounds. One of the heaviest players to ever play in the NBA.
