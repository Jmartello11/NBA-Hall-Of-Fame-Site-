Tom Izzo

Tom Izzo is a college basketaball caoch for the Michigan State Spartans. Izzo has led Michigan State to a great deal of success. He transformed the program into one of the nations top basketball programs. Izzo started out as an assistant coach at Northern Michigan University, and then was an assitant at Michigan State. He was then later named head coach of the Spartans in 1994. Izzos teams are most known for their toughness, and rebounding.
