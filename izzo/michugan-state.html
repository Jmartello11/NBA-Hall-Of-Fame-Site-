Michigan State

Izzo became the coach of the Spartans in 1994. He is the longest tenured coach in the Big Ten Conference. In 1998 his Spartans would make it to the NCAA tournament and begin a 19 consectuive year run of doing so. Izoo would win the NCAA tournament in 2000, and make the Final four 7 times during his time at Michigan. In 2015 Izzo won his 500th career game. The next year in 2016 the Spartans would suffer one of the greatest upsets ever when they would lose to the 15 seeded Middle Tennessee.
